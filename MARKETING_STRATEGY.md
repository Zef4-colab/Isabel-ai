# Marketing Strategy for Isabel AI Chatbot

## TikTok
- **Content Type**: Short video clips showcasing the chatbot in action, user testimonials, and engaging Q&A sessions.
- **Frequency**: Post 3 times a week.
- **Hashtags**: #IsabelAI, #Chatbot, #TechInnovation

## Twitter
- **Content Type**: Quick tips for chatbot usage, user feedback highlights, and links to blog posts.
- **Frequency**: Daily tweets and retweets.
- **Hashtags**: #AI, #Chatbot, #IsabelAI

## LinkedIn
- **Content Type**: In-depth articles about chatbot technology, business applications, and case studies.
- **Frequency**: Weekly posts.
- **Hashtags**: #ArtificialIntelligence, #BusinessInnovation, #IsabelAI

## Instagram
- **Content Type**: Eye-catching visuals of the chatbot interface, infographics, and behind-the-scenes content.
- **Frequency**: 4 posts a week.
- **Hashtags**: #IsabelAI, #Chatbot, #DigitalMarketing

## Conclusion
Combining these strategies across multiple platforms will effectively promote the Isabel AI chatbot and engage potential users.
